Our History

Founded in 2003, R&M Plumbing & Heating has been a trusted name in Bergen County for over 20 years. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the area's most reliable plumbing and heating companies.

We built our reputation on quality workmanship, honest pricing, and treating every customer like family. Our commitment to excellence has earned us thousands of satisfied customers throughout Bergen County and beyond.

As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the unique needs of our community. Whether it's an emergency repair or a planned installation, we're here to serve our neighbors with the same dedication and care we've provided for over two decades.
